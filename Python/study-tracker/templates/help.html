🚀 Getting Started

Study Tracker is a comprehensive tool for coding interview preparation that combines scientific learning techniques with practical features:

🧠 Spaced Repetition

Automatically schedules reviews at optimal intervals (1, 3, 7, 14 days) to maximize retention

⏱️ Time Management

Pomodoro timers with performance tracking to simulate interview conditions

📊 Progress Analytics

Visual insights into your learning patterns and performance trends

🎯 Smart Practice

AI-powered weakness detection and targeted practice recommendations

Quick Setup (5 minutes):

  1. Go to Config to set your study start dates
  2. Visit the Dashboard to see today's topics
  3. Start with the Pomodoro timer for focused sessions
  4. Complete topics and rate your understanding
  5. Check Analytics to track your progress

⭐ Rating System

  • 5 - Very Easy: Mastered, confident

🔬 The Science Behind Study Tracker

🧠 Spaced Repetition Research

Based on Hermann Ebbinghaus's forgetting curve research, the system schedules reviews at scientifically optimal intervals:

  • Day 1: Initial learning
  • Day 3: First review (prevents 50% memory loss)
  • Day 7: Second review (strengthens long-term retention)
  • Day 14: Final review (establishes permanent memory)

⏱️ Interview Simulation Benefits

  • Stress Inoculation: Builds comfort with time pressure
  • Speed Development: Improves problem-solving velocity
  • Performance Tracking: Quantifies interview readiness

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start the 28-day program?

Go to Config and set your start dates for each track. You can start them on different days and activate/deactivate tracks as needed.

Q: What if I miss a day?

No problem! The system is flexible. Just continue with the next topic in sequence. Reviews will automatically catch up.

Q: How does the AI detect weak areas?

The system analyzes your completion ratings. Topics rated 2.5 or below are considered "weak areas" and prioritized for additional practice.

Q: Can I use this for other subjects?

While designed for coding interviews, the curriculum customization allows you to adapt it for any technical subject requiring spaced repetition.

Q: How is this different from just using LeetCode?

Study Tracker adds scientific learning optimization, progress tracking, performance analytics, and structured curriculum progression that pure problem-solving platforms lack.
